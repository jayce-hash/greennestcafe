Our Story

A neighborhood cafe built
to make you feel at home

Green Nest Cafe is Bedford's beloved gathering spot where genuine hospitality meets exceptional coffee. With a 4.9-star rating from hundreds of loyal customers, we've built a space that truly feels like home.

From locally roasted espresso to Korean-inspired YUBU dishes and scratch-made sandwiches — every detail is crafted with intention. Step in, slow down, stay a while.
